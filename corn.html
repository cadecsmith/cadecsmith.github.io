Aflatoxin

Along with a professor at BYU, I researched the spatial correlation between several different factors on risk of aflatoxin contamination in Corn crops in Texas. We found statistically significant results in the relationship between NDVI, Thermal IR reflectance, Temperature, and Precipitation in where aflatoxin affects corn crops. This research was conducted over several months and I had the opportunity to present it at several conferences for undergraduate research.
